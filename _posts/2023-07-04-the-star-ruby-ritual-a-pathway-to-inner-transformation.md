The Ruby Star Ritual, also known as the Star Ruby, is a powerful ceremonial practice within the system of Thelema. Developed by Aleister Crowley, it serves as a purification and banishing ritual, aiming to cleanse the practitioner’s energetic and mental space while invoking protective forces. In this section, we will provide a thorough description of how to perform the Ruby Star Ritual and delve into its specific goals and intentions.

**1. Igniting the Divine Spark:**

  
One of the primary goals of the Ruby Star ritual is to awaken and ignite the divine spark within each individual. The ritual emphasizes the recognition of one’s inherent divinity and the activation of the inner light and power. By connecting with this divine essence, individuals can tap into their true potential, accessing their unique gifts, talents, and abilities. The goal is to inspire a sense of self-empowerment and encourage the expression of one’s authentic self.

**2. Banishing Limiting Beliefs and Energies:**

  
Another important goal of the Ruby Star ritual is to facilitate the release of negative and limiting beliefs, patterns, and energies that hinder personal growth and obstruct the manifestation of one’s True Will. The ritual incorporates banishing techniques to purify the mind, emotions, and energetic field. By letting go of self-imposed limitations and external influences, individuals can create space for new possibilities, expanded consciousness, and transformative experiences.

**3. Protection and Boundaries:**

  
The Ruby Star ritual aims to establish energetic protection and healthy boundaries. In the modern world, where external influences can be overwhelming and disruptive, it is essential to shield oneself from negative energies, distractions, and psychic intrusions. The ritual invokes protective forces to create a sanctuary of personal power and safeguard the practitioner’s energetic well-being. This goal empowers individuals to navigate the challenges of daily life with resilience, clarity, and integrity.

**4. Connecting with Higher Spiritual Forces:**

  
The Ruby Star ritual seeks to foster a connection with higher spiritual forces and tap into the collective wisdom of the universe. By invoking divine energies and archetypal symbols, individuals can establish a link between their personal journey and the larger tapestry of existence. This goal aims to cultivate a sense of interconnectedness, inspiration, and guidance from spiritual realms, facilitating the alignment with one’s True Will and the unfolding of divine purpose.

**5. Cultivating Inner Balance and Integration:**

  
Harmony and balance within oneself are crucial aspects of the Ruby Star ritual. The goal is to integrate the conscious and unconscious aspects of the psyche, harmonizing the different facets of one’s being. This integration supports the development of self-awareness, emotional resilience, and mental clarity. By nurturing inner balance, individuals can make aligned choices, navigate challenges with grace, and effectively express their True Will in all areas of life.

**6. Amplifying Intentions and Manifestation:**

  
The Ruby Star ritual aims to enhance the power of intentions and manifestation. Through focused visualization, concentration, and energy work, individuals can amplify their desires, goals, and intentions. The ritual harnesses the creative potential of the mind and aligns it with the currents of universal energy, increasing the likelihood of successful manifestation. This goal empowers individuals to actively participate in shaping their reality and manifesting their dreams.

Ruby Star ritual encompasses a range of goals that facilitate personal transformation, spiritual growth, and alignment with one’s True Will. Through igniting the divine spark, banishing limiting beliefs, establishing protection and boundaries, connecting with higher spiritual forces, cultivating inner balance, and amplifying intentions, individuals can embark on a path of self-realization, empowerment, and conscious manifestation. The ritual serves as a powerful tool for individuals to navigate the complexities of life and embrace their unique purpose with clarity and authenticity.

Star Ruby is rich in metaphors that hold significance not only within the ritual itself but also in their application to daily life in the 21st century. Let’s explore some of these metaphors and their contemporary relevance:

**1. The Ruby Star:**

  
The image of the radiant ruby star represents the divine spark within each individual. It symbolizes the inner light, power, and potential that resides within us all. In the 21st century, this metaphor reminds us of the importance of recognizing our inherent divinity and embracing our unique gifts and abilities. It encourages us to tap into our inner brilliance and express it authentically in all aspects of our lives.

**2. Banishing and Purification:**

  
The act of banishing in the Ruby Star Ritual serves as a metaphor for letting go of negativity, self-limiting beliefs, and destructive patterns. In the fast-paced and often chaotic modern world, it is crucial to regularly assess and release what no longer serves our highest good. The ritual’s emphasis on purification invites us to cleanse our thoughts, emotions, and environments, creating space for positive growth and transformation.

**3. Protective Energies:**

  
The invocation of protective forces in the ritual represents our innate ability to establish boundaries and safeguard ourselves from external influences. In the 21st century, where we are constantly bombarded with information, opinions, and distractions, it is essential to cultivate discernment and protect our mental and emotional well-being. This metaphor reminds us to consciously choose what we allow into our lives and to prioritize practices that support our mental and energetic hygiene.

**5. The Sign of the Enterer:**

  
The forceful gesture of the Sign of the Enterer in the ritual signifies the assertion of personal will and the power of focused intention. In the 21st century, where distractions are plentiful and our attention is often divided, this metaphor reminds us of the importance of setting clear intentions, maintaining focus, and taking decisive action towards our goals. It encourages us to engage with life purposefully and assertively, aligning our actions with our deepest desires.

**6. Grounding and Integration:**

  
The grounding process in the ritual serves as a metaphor for staying rooted and balanced amidst the rapid pace and technological advancements of the 21st century. It reminds us of the significance of connecting with nature, engaging in mindful practices, and maintaining a sense of inner stability. Grounding ourselves allows us to navigate the challenges of modern life with greater resilience, clarity, and a sense of belonging.

By exploring and applying these metaphors from the Ruby Star Ritual to daily life in the 21st century, we can find inspiration and guidance to live more consciously, authentically, and in alignment with our true selves. These metaphors serve as reminders to nurture our inner light, release what no longer serves us, establish healthy boundaries, create sacred spaces, assert our personal will, and stay grounded amidst the ever-changing world around us.

**Performing the Ruby Star Ritual:**

**1. Preparation:**

  
a. Choose a quiet and private space where you can perform the ritual undisturbed.

  
b. Set up your ceremonial space with a central altar adorned with symbolic objects, such as a representation of the pentagram or the central deity of your choice.

  
c. Prepare the necessary tools, including a wand, a ritual dagger (athame), a cup of water, a censer with incense, and a copy of the ritual text.

**2. Grounding and Centering:**

a. Begin by taking a few moments to ground yourself through deep, intentional breathing. Allow your body and mind to relax, and let go of any distractions or concerns.

**3. The Lesser Banishing Ritual of the Pentagram (LBRP):**

  
a. Perform the LBRP, a preliminary ritual that clears and balances the energetic space. This ritual involves tracing pentagrams in the air with your wand or finger, vibrating sacred names, and visualizing purifying energies.

  
b. The LBRP helps create a protected and purified environment, ready for the subsequent Ruby Star Ritual.

**4. Opening:**

  
a. Stand at the eastern side of your ritual space, facing east. Perform the Sign of the Enterer, thrusting your right hand forward while vibrating the word “Thelema!” Visualize the energy of your will projecting outwards.

  
b. Recite the Opening Invocation, calling upon the divine forces you resonate with, such as the deities of Thelema or your own personal guardian angels or spiritual guides.

**5. The Ruby Star Ritual:**

  
a. Begin by assuming a balanced and stable stance, feet shoulder-width apart.  
b. Perform the Sign of the Enterer again, this time while vibrating the word “Rubaë!” Visualize a radiant ruby star appearing in the space before you.

  
c. Move through the ritual, visualizing each step vividly and feeling the power and presence of the forces invoked. This includes tracing pentagrams with the ritual dagger, vibrating sacred names, and moving through the quarters of the ritual space in a clockwise direction.

  
d. The Ruby Star Ritual involves assertive and commanding gestures, spoken invocations, and visualizations that banish negative influences and invoke protective energies.

  
e. Conclude the ritual by performing the Sign of Silence, closing the door to external influences and sealing the energies raised during the ritual.

**6. Grounding and Integration:**

  
a. Take a moment to ground and integrate the energies raised during the ritual. You can do this by placing your hands on the ground, visualizing any excess energy flowing into the Earth.

  
b. Reflect on your experience, journaling or meditating on any insights, emotions, or visions that may have arisen.
